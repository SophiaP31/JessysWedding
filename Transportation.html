Transportation and Accomodations

Transportation

If you plan on drinking at the reception and need a ride we have rented a bus that will take you from the lake houses to Bandits Ridge after the ceremony. It will be $10 per person and we are limited, so please email us at settlesdownwithpastino@gmail.com so we can add you to the list. It will be a first come basis for seats. There is not Uber or Lyft in the area so please plan accordingly.


The bus will leave from 352 Lakeview Dr Mineral VA at 3:15

The bus will depart the venue after the “send off” at 9:10
